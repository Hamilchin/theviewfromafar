Collapse

The man lies sick with a knotted chest
wishing he could burst into
a violent explosion of sticky-notes
or a silent collapse of honey-like syrup, viscous
or some bang of gunpowdered jelly
or a more-massive-than-thou slow-motion splash
constituent fingered droplet-flowers spreading
a satisfying crash, then draining greedily into pavement
like one tired to the tendons does into a made bed
somehow quenching the thirst of
something greater than him, not him
fooom! ssshhhplash!
no, not him
