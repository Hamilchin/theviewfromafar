Untitled 2

when the moth does it, we complain of

half-promised wind-drops on the evening news

…well folks, don’t let the sunshine fool you…

when the rich do it, we say eat the rich,

we say five-sigma, and blame

the folly of complex financial derivatives

when the cell does it, metastasis we say, and

sorry, there’s nothing more we can do

when the artist does it, genius we say,

and nothing more

and none are ever the wiser

when the atom does it, we call it proliferation,

that uncertain balance of terror,

the damn commies
